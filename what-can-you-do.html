    • What can you do?


    In this section, you will find useful information on how you can reduce your food waste.


    .

    At home.


    • Plan your meals ahead of time
    • Shop with a list and stick to it
    • Store food properly to prolong its shelf life
    • Use up leftovers creatively
    • Compost food scraps

    In life.


    • Buy imperfect produce from farmers markets or grocery stores that sell "ugly" produce
    • Donate excess food to local food banks or charities
    • Use apps like Too Good To Go or Olio to share surplus food with neighbors
    • Join a community garden or start one in your neighborhood
